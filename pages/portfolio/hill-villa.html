Residential Getaway

Hill Villa

A dramatic A-frame design that serves as a luxurious mountain retreat, built with indigenous wood and stone to harmonise with the dramatic Himalayan scenery.

The Site

Perched on a steep slope, the design required deep foundation work and a vertical structure to maximize light and mountain views.

A-Frame Design

The iconic roof manages heavy snowfall while supporting a two-story glass wall, flooding the main living area with light.

Key Features

  • Vaulted, double-height ceilings
  • Wood-burning stove and radiant floor heating
  • Minimalist kitchen designed for entertaining
  • Rooftop solar water heating integration

Hill Villa was designed as the ultimate escape. The interior emphasizes raw, natural textures—local stone walls, exposed timber beams, and soft wool furnishings—creating an atmosphere of quiet, rugged luxury. The large viewing deck provides an unparalleled 180-degree vista of the mountains.
