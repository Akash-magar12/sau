Mixed-Use Development

Doon Boulevard

A pioneering urban project redefining the city center — blending high-end retail, modern workspaces, and luxury residences around a vibrant public plaza.

The Vision

To create a 24/7 urban hub promoting walkability and vibrant social interaction, serving as a commercial and cultural anchor.

Design Strategy

Layered setbacks and terraced greens soften the structure, with natural stone and glass defining a timeless modern aesthetic.

Key Features

  • Multi-level public plaza with seating
  • High-speed elevators for private access
  • Rainwater harvesting & solar systems
  • Pedestrian-friendly pathways & terraces

Doon Boulevard was a complex planning challenge, requiring careful sequencing of commercial, office, and residential components. We ensured that each function maintains its privacy and security while contributing to the overall vibrancy and economy of the development. This project sets a new standard for urban density and mixed-use sustainability.
