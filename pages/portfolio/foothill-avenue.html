Residential Community

Foothill Avenue

An exclusive residential development comprising ten custom-designed villas, masterfully positioned to maximize privacy and views of the surrounding tranquil foothills.

Master Planning

The site plan carefully considers topography and sun paths, ensuring each villa benefits from natural light and unobstructed views while minimizing mutual sightlines.

Villa Design

Each villa blends traditional materials (slate, stone) with modern minimalist forms, featuring expansive outdoor decks and internal courtyards.

Community Amenities

  • Central landscaped gathering space
  • 24/7 guarded security and gated entry
  • Dedicated maintenance and landscaping services
  • Integrated storm water management system

Foothill Avenue is the pinnacle of serene, luxurious living. Our integrated design approach managed the entire process, from environmental assessment and master planning to the detailed interior execution of each unique home. The result is a harmonious community defined by quality and tranquility, maximizing the dramatic natural setting.
